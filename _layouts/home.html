About

Hi, I'm Sarah, a 30-something physician and aspiring web developer. Welcome to my first website! After nine years practicing clinical medicine, I found myself ready to take on a new career challenge. Wanting to pursue my creative side a bit more, I went on the hunt for a career that was equal parts artistic and analytical. Months of research later, I found that perfect combination in front end development and took the leap from medicine to tech.

In August 2016, I started self-teaching with the Skillcrush Front End Developer and Visual Designer blueprints. All the gory details of the learning process and the projects I've done are chronicled over on my blog.

On a personal note, I'm married to a clever guy who also happens to be a developer. We have a couple of tiny humans who we attempt to parent together. My favorite things in life are traveling, summertime, long strolls, strong coffee and seasonal food with perfectly paired wine. I also hold a special place in my heart for ice cream.

I have quite a few Javascript lessons in the queue, but I hope to continue my learning on the job in the near future. If you are an employer and would like to chat, keep scrolling for my contact info!
